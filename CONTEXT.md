# Wang Bang

Wang Bang tracks wedding preparation information across a dashboard, a planning calendar, and a budget ledger.

## Language

**Wedding Schedule Item**:
A calendar entry for wedding preparation on a specific date.
_Avoid_: Wedding task, wedding project

**Dashboard**:
A summary view of wedding preparation, including budget progress and D-day information derived from dates.
_Avoid_: Project overview

**Plan**:
A calendar used only to record dated wedding preparation schedule items.
_Avoid_: Checklist, task board, project plan

**Budget Ledger**:
A ledger of money spent, contracted, paid, and remaining during wedding preparation.
_Avoid_: Project budget
